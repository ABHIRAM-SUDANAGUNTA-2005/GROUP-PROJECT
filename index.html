Web-Based Platform for Managing Student Group Projects

Our Purpose

This platform supports group project management including task assignments, progress tracking, and collaboration tools. It helps students coordinate their work and manage project milestones effectively.

Key Features

Task Assignments

Teachers assign group projects and students coordinate tasks easily.

Progress Tracking

Monitor progress and review project milestones in real-time.

Collabration Tools

Collaboration tools in project management help teams communicate, share resources, and stay aligned to achieve goals efficiently.

For Admin (Teachers)

• Assign group projects
• Monitor progress
• Review final submissions

For Students (Users)

• Coordinate tasks with team
• Track project milestones
• Submit group work
